This animated portfolio was done as a individual final year project. The entire page is filled with animations. The characters are all blinking, the eyes of the building follows the cursor, the windmills are spinning, the birds are pecking on the floor and the soup is always boiling. Background and characteres was done with Adobe Illustrator. The animated portfolio scrolls down to reveal all my previous works and contact info. Clicking on the images of my previous works will show u additional images. There are buttons located at the bottom of the screen to skip to different sections of the page.
